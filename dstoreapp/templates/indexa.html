Our Specialities

  • Customer Satisfaction

    Our customers are at the heart of our business strategy. Hence, we strive our best to see our customers are successful and at every moment we try to add value for our customers with excellence in Technical Services along with a reliable logistical support.

  • Quality Serves

    Our Chemicals and Specialities Business unit serve with niche products addressing the paints and coating industry with UV stabilizers and Anti-Oxidants of highest performance.
